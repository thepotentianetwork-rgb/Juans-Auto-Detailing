Where We Work

Based In Tremonton, UT

Drop your vehicle off at our shop, or have us come to you.

Juan's Auto Detailing is based out of Tremonton, Utah, where our shop sits at 900 W Main St #20. From there, we offer both drop-off service at our location and mobile detailing that comes straight to your home or office. Our mobile service area covers Box Elder County, Cache Valley, Weber County, and Davis County in northern Utah, plus Franklin County in southern Idaho — including the cities of Brigham City, Ogden, Logan, Perry, Layton, and Preston.
